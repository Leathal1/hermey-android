# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Hermey a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and receive constructive technical feedback.
- Focus criticism on ideas, designs, and code rather than people.
- Respect project maintainers' time and scope decisions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing, publishing private information, or threats.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Spam, trolling, or repeated off-topic disruption.
- Misrepresentation of project affiliation or endorsement.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other action when behavior violates this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer. Do not use public issues for sensitive interpersonal or safety reports.
